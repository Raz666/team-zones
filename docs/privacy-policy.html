Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025/12/29

Team Zones respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What the app uses

Team Zones only accesses:

  • The current time of your device
  • The system light/dark appearance setting

This information is used solely to display time correctly and adjust the app's theme.

What the app does not do

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No data is sent over the internet
  • No external APIs or services are called
  • No analytics, tracking, or ads are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data storage

All app data (such as added time zones or names) is stored locally on your device only.
